<p><strong>The Edge emitting Semiconductor Lasers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Distributed Feedback Laser</li><li>Distributed Bragg Reflector Laser</li><li>Fabry-perot Laser</li><li>Broad Area Laser Diodes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Edge emitting semiconductor lasers are electronic devices that emit coherent light through the edge of a semiconductor wafer. There are four main types of edge emitting semiconductor lasers in the market. Distributed Feedback Lasers provide single-frequency operation by incorporating a grating. Distributed Bragg Reflector Lasers use a combination of multiple grating and a cavity to generate narrow linewidths. Fabry-perot Lasers have two parallel reflecting mirrors forming a cavity. Broad Area Laser Diodes are characterized by a wide stripe width that allows for high power, but with broader emission output.</p></p>
