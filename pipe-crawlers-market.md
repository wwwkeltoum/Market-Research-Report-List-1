<p><p>Pipe crawlers are devices used for inspecting and cleaning pipelines in various industries. The market for pipe crawlers can be categorized into three types: SCR, rigid, and flexible. SCR (Superior Conductivity Rubber) pipe crawlers are made of a high-conductivity rubber material, allowing them to navigate through pipes efficiently. Rigid pipe crawlers are sturdy and provide stability during the inspection process. Flexible pipe crawlers are designed with bendable components, enabling them to move around obstacles and navigate through curved pipes easily. Each market type offers unique features, catering to different pipeline inspection requirements.</p></p>
