<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Polypropylene fume hood market offers two main types: ductless fume hoods and ducted fume hoods. Ductless fume hoods are equipped with filters that capture and remove hazardous fumes, making them suitable for spaces where duct installations are not possible. They ensure safety by recirculating purified air. On the other hand, ducted fume hoods are connected to exhaust systems, expelling harmful fumes to the outside environment. They are commonly used in laboratories and industrial settings where continuous ventilation is required. Both types play a crucial role in protecting users from chemical exposure and maintaining air quality.</p></p>
