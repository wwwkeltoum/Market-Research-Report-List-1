<p><strong>The Distributed Acoustic Monitoring Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Monitoring</li><li>Transverse Wave Acoustic Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distributed Acoustic Monitoring Systems (DAS) are used to detect and monitor acoustic waves in various applications. Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Monitoring involves tracking sound waves that move parallel to the direction of wave motion, enabling the system to identify specific sound sources and their locations. Conversely, Transverse Wave Acoustic Monitoring focuses on waves that vibrate perpendicular to the direction of wave motion, providing insights into different vibration patterns. Both types of DAS are utilized for diverse purposes, including industrial monitoring, infrastructure management, and seismic monitoring, among others.</p></p>
