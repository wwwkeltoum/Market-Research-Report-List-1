<p><p>Standard treadmill market types include manual treadmills and motorized treadmills. Manual treadmills are operated by the user's effort, requiring them to walk or run to keep the belt moving. They are generally more affordable and space-efficient. On the other hand, motorized treadmills feature a motor that moves the belt automatically, providing variable speed and incline settings.
