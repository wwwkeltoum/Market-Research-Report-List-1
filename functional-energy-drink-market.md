<p><p>Functional energy drinks are divided into different market types based on their composition and intended purpose. Isotonic energy drinks have a similar concentration of dissolved particles as the body's fluids, making them ideal for hydration during exercise. Hypertonic energy drinks contain a higher concentration of dissolved particles, providing a quick source of energy and aiding in muscle recovery. Hypotonic energy drinks have a lower concentration of dissolved particles, leading to rapid hydration and fluid replenishment. The "others" market may include energy drinks with unique compositions or intended purposes that do not fit into the aforementioned categories.</p></p>
