<p><p>Mill liners are crucial components in mills used in industries such as mining, cement, and power generation. Two common types of mill liners are metal mill liners and rubber mill liners. Metal mill liners are made of high-quality alloys and provide excellent wear resistance, durability, and impact resistance. On the other hand, rubber mill liners are composed of rubber and are more suitable for softer ores and materials as they offer superior shock absorption and noise reduction. Both types of mill liners play a vital role in optimizing mill performance and protecting the mill shell from excessive wear and damage.</p></p>
