<p><p>VCSEL (Vertical Cavity Surface Emitting Laser) is a type of laser diode commonly used in data communication applications. In terms of product type, VCSELs are categorized into single mode and multi-mode. Single mode VCSELs transmit a single beam of light, offering longer reach and higher data rates, while multi-mode VCSELs transmit multiple beams concurrently at shorter distances and lower data rates. They are made of various materials like gallium nitride, gallium arsenide, and indium phosphide. Other markets for VCSELs include applications in sensing, 3D imaging, and industrial heating.</p></p>
