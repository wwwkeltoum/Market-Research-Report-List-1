<p><p>The Small Helical Gear Reducer market comprises various types, including coaxial, orthogonal axis, parallel axis, and other markets. Coaxial gear reducers are designed with parallel axes of input and output shafts, allowing for compact and efficient power transmission. Orthogonal axis gear reducers have input and output shafts positioned at a 90-degree angle, making them suitable for applications that require unique or limited space arrangements. Parallel axis gear reducers feature parallel input and output shafts and are commonly used in heavy-duty applications. The "other" market includes gear reducers that do not fit into the categories mentioned above, such as non-standard or specialized configurations.</p></p>
