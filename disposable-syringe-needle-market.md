<p><p>The disposable syringe needle market consists of two types: conventional syringes and safety syringes. Conventional syringes are the traditional type commonly used in healthcare settings. They consist of a barrel, a plunger, and a detachable needle. Safety syringes, on the other hand, are designed to minimize accidental needlestick injuries. They incorporate features like safety shields or mechanisms that retract the needle after use. These safety syringes offer enhanced protection for both healthcare workers and patients, reducing the risk of transmitting infections or bloodborne diseases.</p></p>
