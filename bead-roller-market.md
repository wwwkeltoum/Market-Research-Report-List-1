<p><p>The Bead Roller market offers two main types of machines: the Manual Ball Machine and the Electric Ball Machine. A Manual Ball Machine is operated by hand, requiring the user to manually guide the metal sheet for bead rolling. On the other hand, an Electric Ball Machine is powered by electricity and automates the process, reducing the need for manual labor. Both machines serve the purpose of creating decorative and functional beads on metal sheets, but differ in terms of operation and level of automation.</p></p>
