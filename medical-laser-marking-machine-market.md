<p><p>The medical laser marking machine market is categorized into several types. Cold laser type machines use low-intensity lasers that do not produce heat, making them suitable for delicate medical procedures. Ultra-short pulsed laser type machines are high-powered and emit ultrashort pulses for precision marking. Fiber laser type machines use fiber-optic technology for fast and precise marking. CO2 laser type machines use carbon dioxide gas lasers for versatile marking on various materials.
