<p><p>Fluid Viscous Dampers (FVD) are devices used to reduce vibrations and absorb energy in mechanical systems. There are two main types of FVD markets: linear type and non-linear type. Linear type dampers provide a constant damping force regardless of the velocity, while non-linear type dampers offer variable damping based on the velocity of the system. Both types of FVDs are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction to improve system stability and reduce structural damage.</p></p>
