<p><p>The Wafer Aligner market is categorized into two types: Vacuum Aligner and Atmosphere Aligner. Vacuum Aligners use a vacuum environment to hold and align wafers, ensuring precise alignment for semiconductor manufacturing processes. On the other hand, Atmosphere Aligners operate in a controlled atmospheric environment without a vacuum, providing a more flexible approach for wafer alignment. Both types play significant roles in the semiconductor industry, enabling efficient and accurate alignment of wafers during the production of various electronic devices.</p></p>
