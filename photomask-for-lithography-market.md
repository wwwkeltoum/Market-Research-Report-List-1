There are various types of photomasks available in the market. Quartz photomasks are made using quartz glass, offering high durability and excellent optical properties. Soda photomasks utilize soda-lime glass, a cost-effective alternative to quartz. Others include alternative materials such as films or plates. Each type serves a specific purpose based on their material composition, optical properties, and cost-effectiveness in lithography processes.</p></p>
