<p><p>Artificial sausage casings refer to the various types of casings used in the production of sausages, substituting natural casings. Cellulose casings are made from cellulose derived from wood pulp and are used for making cooked or smoked sausages. Fibrous casings are made from paper, with an inner layer coated with a cellulose film, and are commonly used for larger sausages. Plastic casings, usually made from polyamide, provide excellent barrier properties and are ideal for boiling or grilling sausages. Other types of artificial casings include collagen casings and non-edible casings made from various materials.</p></p>
