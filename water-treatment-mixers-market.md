<p><p>Water treatment mixers are used in the purification process of water treatment plants. Horizontal water treatment mixers are designed with a horizontal shaft and provide efficient mixing of water and chemicals. They are suitable for large-scale applications. On the other hand, vertical water treatment mixers have a vertical shaft and are used for smaller-scale applications. They provide effective mixing of water and chemicals by creating a vertical flow pattern. Both types play a crucial role in the treatment process by ensuring uniform mixing and distribution of chemicals throughout the water.</p></p>
