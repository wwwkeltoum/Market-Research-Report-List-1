<p><p>Membrane separation technology market includes various types of membrane filtration processes, each serving specific purposes. Reverse osmosis is a widely-used method that removes impurities by forcing water through a semi-permeable membrane. Ultra-filtration is employed to separate larger molecules from liquid solutions, while nano-filtration targets particles even smaller. Micro-filtration is commonly used in water treatment applications. Lastly, there are other membrane separation technologies that cater to niche requirements such as gas separation or electrodialysis. These techniques enable efficient separation processes in industries like water and wastewater treatment, pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and others.</p></p>
