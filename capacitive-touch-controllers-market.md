<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Capacitive Touch Controllers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Touch (Self-Capacitive) Controllers</li><li>Multi-Touch (Mutual Capacitive) Controllers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capacitive touch controllers are used in touchscreens and touch-sensitive devices. Single-touch controllers, also known as self-capacitive controllers, can detect only one touch at a time. They are commonly used in simpler devices like smartphones and tablets. On the other hand, multi-touch controllers, also called mutual capacitive controllers, can detect multiple touches simultaneously. These controllers are used in more advanced devices, allowing users to perform multi-finger gestures like pinch-to-zoom or swipe. Both single-touch and multi-touch controllers cater to different market needs based on the complexity of touch interactions required.</p></p>
