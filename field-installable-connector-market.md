<p><strong>The Field Installable Connector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Type</li><li>Fusion Splice Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Field Installable Connector Market involves two main types: Mechanical Type and Fusion Splice Type. Mechanical Type connectors rely on precise physical alignment to ensure a reliable connection between fibers. They are relatively easy to install and do not require special tools. On the other hand, Fusion Splice Type connectors use a fusion splicing technique to join fibers together. This method provides a low-loss and highly durable connection, making it suitable for more demanding applications. Both types of connectors are used in the field to quickly and conveniently establish fiber-optic connections.</p></p>
