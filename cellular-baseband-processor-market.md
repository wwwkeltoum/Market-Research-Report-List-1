<p><ul><li>Baseband Communication Chip</li><li>Mobile Smart Terminal Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cellular baseband processor market consists of two main types: baseband communication chips and mobile smart terminal chips. Baseband communication chips are responsible for the processing and transmission of wireless signals in cellular devices. They handle tasks such as signal decoding, error correction, and data modulation. On the other hand, mobile smart terminal chips are designed for advanced smartphones and tablets. They provide high-performance processing capabilities, support for multiple wireless technologies, and integration of various functionalities like graphics, multimedia, and connectivity features. Both types of chips are essential for enabling the communication and performance of cellular devices.</p></p>
