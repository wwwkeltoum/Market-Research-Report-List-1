<p><p>- Single Phase Transformer Market: This type of transformer is used in residential and small-scale commercial applications where the demand for electricity is relatively low. It consists of two windings, primary and secondary, which are connected through a magnetic core. Single phase transformers are commonly used to step down voltage from the power grid to a lower level for use in appliances or equipment.</p><p>- Three Phase Transformer Market: This type of transformer is utilized in industrial and large-scale commercial applications where a higher demand for electricity is required. It comprises three sets of primary and secondary windings, connected in either star (wye) or delta configurations. Three phase transformers are primarily used to transfer power at high voltages over long distances and provide electricity to heavy machinery and high-capacity systems.</p></p>
