<p><p>The organic fresh whole white mushroom market consists of three main types: button, medium, and large. Button mushrooms are the smallest variety, typically less than 1.5 inches in diameter. Medium mushrooms fall between 1.5 and 2.5 inches in diameter, while large mushrooms are above 2.5 inches. These distinctions are important as they determine the mushrooms' culinary uses and price range. Buttons are commonly used in salads, soups, and as a pizza topping, while medium and large mushrooms are often preferred for stuffing or grilling due to their larger size and more robust flavor.</p></p>
