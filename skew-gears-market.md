There are different types of skew gears, including Hypoid gears and Worm gears. Hypoid gears have a unique design that allows the shafts to intersect at an offset angle, providing high torque transmission with minimal noise and vibration. On the other hand, Worm gears consist of a cylindrical gear (worm) and a toothed gear (worm wheel), offering high gear ratios and excellent power transmission efficiency.</p></p>
