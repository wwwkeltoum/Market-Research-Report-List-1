<p><p>The Medical Vacuum Station market is segmented into two types: Vertical Pumps Configuration and Horizontal Pumps Configuration. In Vertical Pumps Configuration, the vacuum pumps are installed vertically, with the suction and discharge outlets aligned vertically. This configuration is suitable for limited floor space. In Horizontal Pumps Configuration, the vacuum pumps are installed horizontally, with the suction and discharge outlets aligned horizontally. This configuration is ideal for applications where more than one pump is needed or where space is not a constraint. Both configurations serve the purpose of generating medical vacuum for various healthcare applications.</p></p>
