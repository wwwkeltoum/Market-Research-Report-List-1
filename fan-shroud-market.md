<p><p>A fan shroud is an integral component of a cooling system in vehicles. It is a cover that surrounds the electric fan and is designed to improve the efficiency of the cooling process by directing the airflow through the radiator. Fan shrouds help in creating a vacuum effect that draws more air through the radiator, thus enhancing the overall cooling capacity of the system.</p><p>The global fan shroud market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The market is driven by factors such as increasing vehicle production, growing emphasis on improving fuel efficiency, and rising demand for electric vehicles. Additionally, the rising need for advanced cooling systems to meet the stringent emission standards and regulations is also propelling the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing adoption of electric and hybrid vehicles is expected to boost the demand for fan shrouds. These vehicles require efficient cooling systems to manage the heat generated from the electric components, further driving the market growth.
