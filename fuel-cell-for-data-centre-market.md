<p><p>The fuel cell for data centre market can be classified into various types. Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) fuel cells use a solid polymer membrane and are suitable for small to medium-sized data centres. Solid Oxide Fuel Cells (SOFC) operate at high temperatures and are ideal for large-scale data centres. Molten Carbonate fuel cells are capable of high efficiency and can handle a wide range of temperatures. Phosphoric Acid Fuel Cells are well-suited for both large and medium-sized data centres. Lastly, there are other fuel cell types that cater to specific requirements and niche markets within the data centre industry.</p></p>
