PVC jars, made from polyvinyl chloride, are resistant to chemicals and are often used for storing corrosive substances. PP jars, made from polypropylene, have high resistance to heat and are suitable for chemical storage. PS jars, made from polystyrene, offer good clarity and are commonly used for cosmetic and personal care products.
