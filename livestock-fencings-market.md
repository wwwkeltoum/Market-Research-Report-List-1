<p><p>Livestock Fencing is classified into two main types: Electrical Fencing and Non-electrical Fencing. Electrical fencing uses a low electrical current to deter animals from crossing or damaging the fence, providing a psychological deterrent rather than physical barriers. Non-electrical fencing, on the other hand, relies on traditional materials like wood or wire to create physical barriers. These fences are more durable and suitable for securing large livestock, as they offer a more substantial obstacle to prevent animals from escaping or predators from entering.</p></p>
