<p><p>The ready-to-eat cereals market consists of two main types: pure cereals and composite cereals. Pure cereals are made solely from one type of grain, such as wheat or oats. They typically offer a specific flavor and texture to appeal to consumers seeking a simple breakfast option. On the other hand, composite cereals are a mix of different types of grains, nuts, dried fruits, and other ingredients. They offer a more diverse taste and nutritional profile, targeting consumers looking for a variety of flavors and added health benefits.</p></p>
