<p><p>The Automatic Lathe Market can be categorized into two types: Horizontal Lathe and Vertical Lathe. Horizontal lathes are designed with the axis of rotation parallel to the ground, allowing the workpiece to spin on a horizontal plane. On the other hand, vertical lathes have a spindle axis that is perpendicular to the ground, enabling the workpiece to rotate on a vertical plane. Both types offer efficient and precise machining capabilities, but their orientations differ, catering to specific applications and needs in the manufacturing industry.</p></p>
