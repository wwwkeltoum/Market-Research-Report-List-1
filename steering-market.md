<p><strong>The Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS)</li><li>Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS)</li><li>Manual</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steering market types refer to different systems used to control the movement of a vehicle. Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS) combines electrical and hydraulic systems to assist the driver in steering. Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS) uses only hydraulic pressure to aid in steering. Manual steering, on the other hand, requires the driver to exert physical force to control the direction of the vehicle. These market types offer varying levels of assistance and differ in their mechanisms of operation, resulting in different driving experiences.</p></p>
