<p><p>Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disorder (COPD) is a chronic inflammatory lung disease that causes obstructed airflow from the lungs. It is a progressive disease that worsens over time and can significantly impact an individual's quality of life.</p><p>The global market for COPD treatment is experiencing steady growth, driven primarily by the rising prevalence of this disease. Methylxanthines help open the airways and improve breathing. Long-acting bronchodilators provide sustained relief over an extended period.
