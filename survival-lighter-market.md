<p><p>Survival lighters are devices specifically designed for outdoor and emergency situations. Electric lighters are rechargeable via USB and produce an electric arc, making them windproof and reliable in various weather conditions. Fuel lighters, on the other hand, rely on traditional butane or lighter fluid to create a flame. They require refilling and can be affected by wind. Plasma lighters utilize electrical energy to create a high-temperature plasma arc instead of a traditional flame. They are windproof, waterproof, and require charging. Each type offers different advantages and considerations for survival scenarios.</p></p>
