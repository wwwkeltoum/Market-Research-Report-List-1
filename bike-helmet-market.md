<p><p>A bike helmet is a protective headgear worn by cyclists to minimize the risk of head injuries in the event of a crash or accident. It is designed to absorb and distribute the impact energy, reducing the force applied to the skull and brain. Bike helmets typically consist of a hard outer shell made of polycarbonate or ABS plastic and a foam liner made of expanded polystyrene (EPS) or expanded polypropylene (EPP).</p><p>The future of the bike helmet market appears promising and is expected to witness substantial growth over the forecasted period. The rising awareness regarding road safety and the increasing number of bike riders, both for transportation and recreational purposes, are key factors driving the demand for bike helmets. Additionally, governments and regulatory bodies across the globe are imposing strict regulations regarding the use of helmets, further boosting the market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in technology have led to the development of smart bike helmets. These helmets come equipped with various features such as built-in lights, turn signals, rearview cameras, and Bluetooth connectivity, enhancing rider safety and convenience.
