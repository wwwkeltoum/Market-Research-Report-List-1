<p><strong>The Home Fire Sprinklers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet</li><li>Dry</li><li>Preaction</li><li>Deluge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Home fire sprinkler systems are designed to provide immediate response in case of fire, preventing its spread and minimizing potential damage. There are four types of home fire sprinkler systems available in the market, each with its own characteristics. Wet systems have pressurized water in the pipes ready to instantly extinguish the fire. Dry systems use compressed air instead of water until the sprinkler is activated, preventing freezing in colder regions. Preaction systems require two triggers before water release, typically used in areas at risk of accidental activation. Deluge systems release water from all sprinklers simultaneously, suitable for areas with highly flammable materials or hazards requiring immediate flood-like response.</p></p>
