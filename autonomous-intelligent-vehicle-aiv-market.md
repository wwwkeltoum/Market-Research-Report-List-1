<p><p>The market for Autonomous Intelligent Vehicles (AIV) encompasses various types such as SCARA, Delta, Cartesian, Articulated, Collaborative, and Dual Arm. SCARA robots are ideal for assembly and pick-and-place operations. Delta robots excel in high-speed packaging and sorting tasks. Cartesian robots have a three-axis linear movement, suitable for precise and repetitive operations. Articulated robots have multiple joints resembling human arms, used in a wide range of applications. Collaborative robots assist human workers in tasks requiring human-like dexterity and precision. Dual Arm robots have two articulated arms for more complex and coordinated operations.</p></p>
