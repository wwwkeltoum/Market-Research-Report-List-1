<p><p>Horizontal CNC Boring Mill market is segmented into three types: 3-axis, 5-axis, and 4-axis machines. In a 3-axis machine, the tool moves along the X, Y, and Z axes, allowing for cutting in three directions. A 5-axis machine enables cutting in five axes, adding rotation along the X, Y, and Z axes. This offers greater flexibility in machining complex geometries. Likewise, a 4-axis machine has an additional rotation along one axis, enabling the cutting tool to access more angles and perform intricate operations. These varied market types cater to different manufacturing needs and offer differing levels of precision and versatility.</p></p>
