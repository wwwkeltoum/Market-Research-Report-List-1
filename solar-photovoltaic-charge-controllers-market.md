<p><p>Solar Photovoltaic Charge Controllers are used to regulate the voltage and current from solar panels to the battery bank. There are two main types- PWM Type and MPPT Type. PWM Type controllers are cost-effective and suitable for small-scale applications. MPPT Type controllers are more efficient and can increase the energy harvest from the solar panels by up to 30%. They are ideal for larger systems and areas with variable sunlight conditions. Both types serve the purpose of optimizing power output and protecting the battery bank.</p></p>
