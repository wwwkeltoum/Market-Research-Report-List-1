<p><p>Surface Mount Resistors are electronic components used to limit the flow of electric current in a circuit. These resistors are mounted directly onto the surface of a printed circuit board (PCB) using solder, which eliminates the need for drilling holes or inserting leads. They are preferred over through-hole resistors, as they save space, reduce manufacturing costs, and offer better electrical and thermal performance.</p><p>The Surface Mount Resistors Market is witnessing significant growth, primarily driven by the increasing adoption of electronic devices across various industries such as consumer electronics, automotive, telecommunication, and healthcare.
