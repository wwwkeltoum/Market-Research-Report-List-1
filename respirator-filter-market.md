<p><p>The respirator filter market comprises three main types: air filters, gas filters, and liquid filters. Air filters are designed to remove particulate matter, such as dust or pollen, from the air we breathe. Gas filters, on the other hand, are used to trap and neutralize harmful gases and vapors, such as chemicals or fumes. Lastly, liquid filters function to purify liquids, removing impurities or contaminants that may be harmful to human health when ingested or exposed through the respiratory system.</p></p>
