<p><p>Reusable packaging refers to packaging materials that can be used multiple times before being discarded. The market for reusable packaging is categorized into various types. Pallets are flat structures used to stack and transport goods. Crates are rigid containers used for packaging and shipping goods. Intermediate Bulk Containers (IBCs) are large containers used to store and transport liquids or granular materials. Drums and barrels are cylindrical containers primarily used for storing liquids. Bottles are standard containers for storing liquids or small items. Dunnage refers to materials used as cushioning or support in packaging. The "Others" category includes various additional types of reusable packaging materials in the market.</p></p>
