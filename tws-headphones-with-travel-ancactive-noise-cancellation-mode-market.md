<p><p>TWS headphones with Travel ANC (Active Noise Cancellation) mode are available in two different market types: Passive Noise Reduction and Active Noise Cancellation. </p><p>Passive Noise Reduction headphones block out external noise by physically blocking sound waves through materials like foam or rubber. While they can reduce some ambient noise, they do not actively cancel it.</p><p>On the other hand, Active Noise Cancellation headphones use built-in microphones to pick up external sounds and generate anti-noise signals. These signals cancel out the incoming noise, providing a more immersive and noise-free listening experience.</p></p>
