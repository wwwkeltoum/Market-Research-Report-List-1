<p><ul><li>Fibrin Specific Drugs</li><li>Non-Fibrin Specific Drugs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Clot buster drugs are medications used to dissolve blood clots and restore blood flow. There are two main types in the market. Fibrin specific drugs directly target the fibrin component of the clot, helping to break it down and dissolve it. On the other hand, non-fibrin specific drugs work by activating plasminogen, an enzyme that leads to the breakdown of both fibrin and fibrinogen in the clot. Both types of clot buster drugs play a crucial role in treating conditions such as heart attacks and strokes by restoring blood circulation.</p></p>
