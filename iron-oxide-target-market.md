<p><p>The iron oxide target market consists of two main types: plane target and rotating target. Plane target refers to the utilization of iron oxide in a fixed, stationary position during a deposition process. This type is commonly used in thin film coating applications. On the other hand, rotating target involves the use of a spinning iron oxide target for deposition. This type is often employed in larger-scale manufacturing processes where higher deposition rates and increased material utilization are desired.
