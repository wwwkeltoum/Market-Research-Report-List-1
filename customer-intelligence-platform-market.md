<p><p>The Customer Intelligence Platform (CIP) market can be classified into two types: Cloud Based and On-Premise. A Cloud Based CIP refers to a software solution that is hosted on the internet and accessed through a web browser. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as data is stored in the cloud. On the other hand, an On-Premise CIP is a solution installed and maintained on the customer's own servers or infrastructure. It provides maximum control and security but requires upfront investment and ongoing maintenance expenses. The choice between these options depends on the organization's specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
