<p><p>Connected packaging can be defined as packaging that integrates digital technology to enhance consumer experience, ensure product security, and enable efficient supply chain management. It involves the use of elements such as QR codes, RFID tags, or sensors embedded in packaging materials to connect with smartphones or other devices.</p><p>The connected packaging market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.5% during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, the rising demand for improved consumer engagement and demand for product information is driving the adoption of connected packaging. Connected packaging allows manufacturers to provide consumers with interactive experiences, product details, and promotions.</p><p>Secondly, the need for product authentication and anti-counterfeiting measures is driving the demand for connected packaging. By integrating digital technology like QR codes or RFID tags, manufacturers can track products throughout the supply chain, ensuring the authenticity of the product and minimizing fraud.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing focus on sustainability is also expected to drive the connected packaging market. Connected packaging technologies enable efficient supply chain management, reducing wastage and optimizing resources. Additionally, connected packaging can help in inventory management and preventing product expiration, reducing food and product waste.</p><p>In terms of trends, the use of augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR) technologies in connected packaging is gaining popularity. Brands are utilizing AR/VR to provide immersive experiences and product demonstrations to consumers.</p><p>Another emerging trend in connected packaging is the integration of Internet of Things (IoT) technology. IoT-enabled packaging allows real-time tracking of products, ensuring timely delivery and efficient supply chain management.</p><p>Overall, the connected packaging market is witnessing significant growth, driven by consumer demand for enhanced experiences, product security, and sustainable practices in packaging.</p></p>
