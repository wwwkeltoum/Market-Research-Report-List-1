Telescoping boom lifts are characterized by a straight extendable arm that reaches high heights vertically while maintaining stability. In contrast, articulated boom lifts feature multiple jointed sections that allow for increased maneuverability and access to hard-to-reach areas. Both types of electric boom lifts use electric motors powered by rechargeable batteries, making them environmentally friendly and quieter compared to diesel-powered counterparts. They are commonly utilized in various industries for their efficient and safe elevated work capabilities.</p></p>
