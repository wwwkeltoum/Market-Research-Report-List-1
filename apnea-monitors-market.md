<p><ul><li>Single Channel Screening Devices</li><li>Actigraphy Systems</li><li>PSG Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Apnea Monitors are devices used to monitor sleep apnea, a sleep disorder where breathing repeatedly stops and starts. The market types include Single Channel Screening Devices, Actigraphy Systems, and PSG (Polysomnography) Devices. Single Channel Screening Devices are simple monitors that record a single physiological parameter like chest wall movement. Actigraphy Systems use a wristwatch-like device to measure movement and detect sleep patterns. PSG Devices are advanced systems that record multiple physiological parameters like brain waves, eye movement, and oxygen levels, providing comprehensive sleep assessments. These different market types cater to varying needs and levels of sleep disorder diagnosis and monitoring.</p></p>
