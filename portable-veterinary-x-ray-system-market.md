<p><p>The portable veterinary X-ray system market is divided into two major types: digital and analog. Digital systems use sensors to capture X-ray images, which are then converted into digital format for viewing and analysis. Digital systems offer advantages such as enhanced image quality, ease of image manipulation, and the ability to store and share images electronically. Analog systems, on the other hand, use traditional X-ray film to capture images. While analog systems are less expensive, they require the use of chemicals for image development and offer limited image manipulation capabilities.</p></p>
