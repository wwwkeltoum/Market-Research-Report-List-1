<p><strong>The Boats and Yachts Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Actual Cash Value</li><li>Agreed Amount Value</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Boats and yachts insurance market offers two primary types of coverage: actual cash value (ACV) and agreed amount value (AAV). ACV coverage considers the current market value of the vessel at the time of damage or loss, accounting for depreciation. In contrast, AAV coverage involves reaching an agreed-upon value for the boat with the insurer prior to policy initiation. This ensures that in the event of a claim, the predetermined value is paid, regardless of depreciation. These market types cater to different needs and circumstances in boat and yacht insurance.</p></p>
