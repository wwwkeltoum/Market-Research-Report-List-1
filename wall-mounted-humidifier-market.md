<p><ul><li>Vapor Type Humidifiers</li><li>Water Spray Humidifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wall-mounted humidifiers come in two main types: vapor type humidifiers and water spray humidifiers. Vapor type humidifiers use heat to create steam, which is then released into the air to increase humidity levels. Water spray humidifiers, on the other hand, release finely atomized water droplets into the air to achieve the same effect. Both types of humidifiers are designed to improve indoor air quality and alleviate symptoms of dryness, such as dry skin and respiratory issues.</p></p>
