<p><p>Disposable eyelid retractors are medical tools used during eye surgery to hold back the eyelids and provide better access to the eyes. There are three main types of disposable eyelid retractors in the market: manual, automatic, and semi-automatic. Manual retractors require manual adjustment and positioning by the user. Automatic retractors are self-adjusting and provide consistent tension. Semi-automatic retractors require some manual adjustment but also have automated features. Each type offers different levels of ease of use, precision, and control during eye surgeries.</p></p>
