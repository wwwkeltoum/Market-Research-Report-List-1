<p><p>The algae extract market consists of two main types: seaweed and microalgae. Seaweed extracts are derived from various types of seaweed, such as kelp or dulse, and are commonly used in food and beverage products, animal feed, cosmetics, and fertilizers. On the other hand, microalgae extracts are obtained from microscopic algae species like spirulina or chlorella, and they have a wide range of applications, including dietary supplements, biofuel production, pharmaceuticals, and wastewater treatment. Both seaweed and microalgae extracts are gaining popularity due to their nutritional value and potential ecological benefits.</p></p>
