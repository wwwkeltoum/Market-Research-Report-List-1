<p><p>A Cloud Point of Sale (POS) system is a technology that allows businesses to manage their sales transactions, inventory, and customer data using cloud-based software and hardware. Firstly, the increasing adoption of cloud technology across industries has led to a greater demand for cloud-based POS systems, as they offer greater flexibility, scalability, and cost-efficiency compared to legacy systems.
