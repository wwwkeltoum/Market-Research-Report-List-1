<p><p>Enterprise Information Archiving (EIA) is a practice used by organizations to store and retain large amounts of data, including emails, documents, and multimedia files. The EIA market can be categorized into three types: </p><p>1. On-Premises: Companies deploy and manage their archiving systems within their own infrastructure, maintaining complete control over their data and security.</p><p>2. Managed: Organizations outsource their archiving requirements to a third-party provider who oversees the storage, management, and security of their data, freeing up internal resources.</p><p>3. Hybrid: This approach combines on-premises and managed archiving, allowing organizations to store some data on their premises while leveraging external providers for additional storage and management needs.</p></p>
