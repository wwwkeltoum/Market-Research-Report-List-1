<p><ul><li>Spring-loaded Safety Valves</li><li>Pilot-operated Safety Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Safety Valves Market comprises two main types: Spring-loaded Safety Valves and Pilot-operated Safety Valves. Spring-loaded Safety Valves are designed to automatically release excess pressure in industrial equipment to prevent damage or explosions. These valves are activated by a spring that is set to a specific pressure threshold. On the other hand, Pilot-operated Safety Valves use a pilot valve to control the opening and closing of the main valve. This type of valve is typically used in applications where large volumes of fluids or gases need to be released safely.</p></p>
