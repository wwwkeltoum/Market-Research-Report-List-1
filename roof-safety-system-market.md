<p><p>The roof safety system market encompasses various types of safety equipment used to ensure the safety of workers and individuals working at heights. These include guardrails, which are barriers installed along the edges of rooftops to prevent falls. Fall arrest equipment, such as harnesses and lifelines, are used to restrain a person in the event of a fall. Sunroof screens are protective coverings that prevent falls through skylights or roof openings. Other types of safety systems may include anchor points, access ladders, and safety nets.</p></p>
