<p><p>The refrigeration temperature monitoring system market offers two types of systems - wired and wireless. A wired system consists of sensors connected to a central monitoring unit via physical cables, allowing real-time temperature monitoring and data recording. On the other hand, a wireless system employs wireless sensors that transmit temperature data to a central unit without the need for cables, offering greater flexibility and ease of installation. Both systems play a crucial role in ensuring optimal refrigeration conditions and preventing product spoilage in various industries such as food and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
