<p><p>Mechanical isolators are devices used to reduce the transmission of vibrations and shocks between two systems. They come in various shapes including spiral, dish, ring, plate, and others. </p><p>Spiral shape isolators consist of multiple interconnected spiral springs that provide high load capacity and absorb vibrations.</p><p>Dish shape isolators have a concave shape that allows them to support heavy loads and dampen vibrations effectively.</p><p>Ring shape isolators are circular in shape and typically made of rubber or metal, providing both isolation and support for the equipment.</p><p>Plate shape isolators are flat, rigid plates that offer high load-bearing capacity and minimize vibrations.</p><p>Other types of mechanical isolators include cone shape, rectangular shape, and cylindrical shape, each designed to meet specific application requirements.</p></p>
