<p><p>Blood Bag Systems are specialized bags made of plastic that are used for collection, storage, and transfusion of blood and blood components. One of the major trends is the adoption of integrated blood bag systems, which include multiple components such as blood bags, tubes, and needle holders, in a single unit. This integrated approach eliminates the need for multiple devices, reduces the risk of contamination, and enhances the overall efficiency of blood collection and transfusion processes.</p><p>Another trend is the increasing adoption of disposable blood bag systems over reusable ones. Single blood bag systems consist of one bag for collecting and storing blood. Double blood bag systems comprise two bags, used for separating components like red blood cells and plasma.
