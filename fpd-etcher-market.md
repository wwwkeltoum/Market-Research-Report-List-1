<p><p>The FPD Etcher market consists of two main types: Dry Etcher and Wet Etcher. Dry Etcher uses gas plasma to etch the surface of a material, primarily for semiconductor manufacturing. It offers precise and intricate etching results, making it suitable for delicate processes. On the other hand, Wet Etcher employs chemicals or solutions to remove material from the surface, commonly used in industries like printed circuit board fabrication. Wet Etcher offers faster etching speeds but may require more frequent maintenance due to chemical usage. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, enabling efficient and accurate material etching.</p></p>
