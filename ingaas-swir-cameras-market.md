<p><p>The InGaAs SWIR Cameras Market comprises two main types: SWIR Area Cameras and SWIR Linear Cameras. SWIR Area Cameras capture images over a broad area, providing high-resolution visuals, making them suitable for applications like surveillance and machine vision. On the other hand, SWIR Linear Cameras capture images line by line, providing continuous imaging for applications such as sorting and quality inspection. Both types of cameras utilize InGaAs technology, which enables them to operate in the short-wave infrared spectrum, making them ideal for low light conditions and materials penetration.</p></p>
