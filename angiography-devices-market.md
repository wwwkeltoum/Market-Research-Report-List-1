<p><p>Angiography devices are medical tools used to visualize blood vessels in the body for diagnosis and treatment of various conditions. The market for these devices can be categorized into three types: X-Ray Angiography Devices, CT Angiography Devices, and MR Angiography Devices. X-Ray angiography devices use X-ray technology to produce detailed images of blood vessels. CT angiography devices combine X-ray and computer technology to create detailed 3D images. MR angiography devices use magnetic fields and radio waves to generate detailed images without the use of radiation. These devices play a crucial role in diagnosing and guiding treatment for cardiovascular diseases.</p></p>
