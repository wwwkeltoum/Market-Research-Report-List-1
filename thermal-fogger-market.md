<p><p>The Thermal Fogger market can be broadly categorized into three types: Gas Foggers, Fuel Foggers, and Electric Foggers. Gas Foggers use a propane or butane gas cylinder to generate heat, which produces the fog. Fuel Foggers utilize diesel or gasoline as their fuel source to generate the required heat. On the other hand, Electric Foggers rely on electricity to generate heat for fog creation. These different market types provide options for users based on their preference of fuel source and power supply.</p></p>
