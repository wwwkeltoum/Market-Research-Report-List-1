<p><p>The travel cots market includes two main types: folding type and shell type. Folding travel cots are designed to be portable and can easily be collapsed for storage or transportation. These cots typically have a compact structure that can be folded into a smaller size. On the other hand, shell type travel cots have a fixed structure with a solid frame and rigid walls.
