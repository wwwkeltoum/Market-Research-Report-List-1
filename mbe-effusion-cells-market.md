<p><strong>The MBE Effusion Cells Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Temperature Effusion Cells</li><li>Medium Temperature Effusion Cells</li><li>High Temperature Effusion Cells</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MBE Effusion Cells market can be categorized into three types based on the temperature at which they operate: Low Temperature Effusion Cells, Medium Temperature Effusion Cells, and High Temperature Effusion Cells. Low Temperature cells operate at lower temperatures, typically below 400 degrees Celsius, and are used for materials that require a gentle deposition process. Medium Temperature cells operate at temperatures ranging from 400 to 900 degrees Celsius, offering a wider range of materials for deposition. High Temperature cells operate at temperatures above 900 degrees Celsius, allowing for deposition of highly refractory materials or metals that require higher melting points.</p></p>
