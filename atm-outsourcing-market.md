<p><p>ATM outsourcing market applications can be divided into two modes: in-bank and off-bank. In the in-bank mode, ATMs are owned and operated by the bank itself. These ATMs provide banking services exclusively to the bank's customers. On the other hand, in the off-bank mode, ATMs are managed by third-party service providers and offer financial services to customers of multiple banks. This mode allows banks to reduce costs and expand their ATM network without investing in additional infrastructure.</p></p>
