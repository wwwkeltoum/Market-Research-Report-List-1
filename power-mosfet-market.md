<p><strong>The Power MOSFET Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Depletion Mode Power MOSFET</li><li>Enhancement Mode Power MOSFET</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power MOSFET market includes two main types: Depletion Mode Power MOSFET and Enhancement Mode Power MOSFET. The Depletion Mode Power MOSFET is normally conducting with zero input voltage and requires a negative voltage to control its state of operation. On the other hand, the Enhancement Mode Power MOSFET is off by default and requires a positive voltage to be applied to its gate to conduct. Both types are widely used in various applications and industries for their unique characteristics and capabilities.</p></p>
