<p><p>Individual Quick Freezing (IQF) tunnel freezer is a type of freezer that is used to freeze food products individually. This freezing process involves passing the food products through a series of freezing chambers, where they are exposed to a very low temperature and a high-velocity airflow. This results in the quick freezing of the products without forming ice crystals, thus preserving the flavor, texture, and nutritional value of the food.</p><p>The IQF tunnel freezer market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
