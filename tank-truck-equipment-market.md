<p><p>In the tank truck equipment market, there are two main types: mechanical and electrical. Mechanical equipment refers to components and systems that are operated manually or mechanically, such as valves, pumps, and pressure gauges. These equipment are controlled by physical manipulation and do not require any electrical power. On the other hand, electrical equipment includes components that rely on electrical power to operate, such as sensors, monitors, and control panels. These devices use electricity to perform functions such as measuring fluid levels, monitoring temperatures, and controlling system operations.</p></p>
