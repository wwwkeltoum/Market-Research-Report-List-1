<p><ul><li>Subterranean Termite Monitoring Service</li><li>Dry wood Termite Monitoring Service</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Termite monitoring services can be categorized into two main types: subterranean termite monitoring service and dry wood termite monitoring service. Subterranean termite monitoring service focuses on detecting and preventing infestations by termites that live in the soil. It involves placing monitoring stations in the ground to attract and trap termites. On the other hand, dry wood termite monitoring service primarily targets termites that infest wood structures. This type of service typically includes the use of specialized equipment to detect termite activity within the wooden components of a building.</p></p>
