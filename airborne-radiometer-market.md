<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The airborne radiometer market consists of two main types: Millimeter Wave Radiometer and Interferometric Correlator Radiometer. Millimeter Wave Radiometers measure the electromagnetic spectrum in the millimeter wave frequency range, enabling analysis of atmospheric properties such as humidity and temperature. Interferometric Correlator Radiometers use interferometry techniques to measure the differences in phase between received signals, allowing for high-precision mapping of the Earth's surface. Both types are commonly used in airborne applications such as weather forecasting, climate research, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
