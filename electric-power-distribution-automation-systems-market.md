<p><p>Electric power distribution automation systems refer to the set of technologies and solutions used to automate various aspects of power distribution. The market types in this sector include substation automation systems, feeder automation systems, consumer side automation systems, and others. Substation automation systems involve automating the monitoring and control of substations. Feeder automation systems focus on automating the operation of power distribution feeders. Consumer side automation systems aim to automate functions such as metering and load control at the consumer end. The "others" category includes any additional automation systems employed in power distribution.</p></p>
