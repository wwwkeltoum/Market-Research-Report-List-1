<p><p>RDBMS (Relational Database Management System) software is available in two main market types: Cloud-based and On-Premise. Cloud-based RDBMS software is hosted and accessed over the internet, allowing users to store and manage their databases remotely, without requiring physical servers. On the other hand, On-Premise RDBMS software is installed and managed locally on physical servers, giving organizations direct control over their databases. Both options have their benefits and considerations, with cloud-based offering scalability, flexibility, and reduced maintenance, while on-premise provides increased security and data control.</p></p>
