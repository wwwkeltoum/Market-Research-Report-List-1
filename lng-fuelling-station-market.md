<p><p>The LNG fuelling station market consists of two main types: mobile stations and permanent stations. Mobile stations are designed to be moved easily and can be deployed to various locations as needed. They are often used in areas with temporary energy demands, such as construction sites or events. Permanent stations, on the other hand, are fixed installations that provide a continuous supply of LNG. They are typically established in areas with regular energy requirements, such as ports or transportation hubs. Both types serve the purpose of delivering LNG fuel for vehicles or other applications, but differ in their mobility and flexibility.</p></p>
