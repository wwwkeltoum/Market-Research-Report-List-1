<p><p>The torque gauge market consists of two main types: handheld torque gauge and tabletop torque gauge. Handheld torque gauges are compact and portable devices used for measuring torque directly on a wide range of applications. They are lightweight and easy to handle, making them suitable for fieldwork and on-site inspections. On the other hand, tabletop torque gauges are larger, stationary devices commonly used in manufacturing and laboratory environments. They offer higher precision and accuracy for torque testing and are typically more expensive than handheld gauges.</p></p>
