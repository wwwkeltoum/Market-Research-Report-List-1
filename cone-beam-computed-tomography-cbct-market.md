<p><p>Cone Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT) is a specialized type of medical imaging technology used for producing detailed 3D images of the body. The two main types of detectors used in CBCT machines are Image Intensifier Detector and Flat-Panel Imager Detector. Image Intensifier Detector utilizes a fluorescent screen to convert X-ray into visible light, while Flat-Panel Imager Detector uses a flat sensor to directly capture X-ray images. Both detectors have their own advantages and applications in the CBCT market.</p></p>
