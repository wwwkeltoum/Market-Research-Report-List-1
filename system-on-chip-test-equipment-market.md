<p><p>The System-on-Chip (SOC) Test Equipment Market can be classified into two types: Automatic SOC Test Equipment and Semi-Automatic SOC Test Equipment. </p><p>Automatic SOC Test Equipment refers to testing devices that can perform tests and measurements on SOCs automatically, without the need for manual intervention. These systems are equipped with advanced technologies and algorithms to efficiently and accurately test the functionality, performance, and quality of SOCs.</p><p>On the other hand, Semi-Automatic SOC Test Equipment requires some level of manual intervention during the testing process. These systems usually involve a combination of automated test procedures and manual tasks, where operators may need to configure test setups, load and unload devices, or interpret certain test results manually.</p><p>Both types of SOC test equipment cater to the needs of different users based on their specific requirements, level of complexity, and budget constraints for testing SOC devices.</p></p>
