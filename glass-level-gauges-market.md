<p><p>Glass level gauges are used to measure the liquid level in industrial processes. There are two main types: glass plate/tube level gauges and magnetic float level gauges. Glass plate/tube level gauges consist of transparent glass plates or tubes that allow visual observation of the liquid level. Magnetic float level gauges use a magnetic float inside a tube, which rises or falls with the liquid level and is detected by a magnetic sensor. Both types provide accurate and reliable measurements of liquid levels in various industries.</p></p>
