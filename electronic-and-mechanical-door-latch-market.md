<p><strong>The Electronic and Mechanical Door Latch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronic Door Latch</li><li>Mechanical Door Latch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Door Latch refers to a technologically advanced locking system that operates electronically, using a combination of sensors, electric motors, and microcontrollers. It offers features like keyless entry through keypad or biometric authentication, remote access control, and integration with smart home systems. On the other hand, the Mechanical Door Latch is a traditional locking mechanism that operates manually, using mechanical components like levers, springs, and latches. It requires physical interaction like turning a key or pulling a lever to open or close the door. Both types serve the purpose of securing doors but differ in terms of technology and functionality.</p></p>
