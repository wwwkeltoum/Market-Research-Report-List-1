<p><p>The LED thermal products market includes various types of products designed to dissipate heat generated by LEDs. Heat sinks are metallic components that transfer heat away from the LED. Ceramic PCBs are printed circuit boards made of ceramic material, which have excellent thermal conductivity. Fansinks combine heat sinks with fans to enhance heat dissipation. Thermal clad boards have a metal base for efficient heat transfer. Thermally conductive pads are used to fill gaps between LED and heat sinks to improve thermal performance. Finally, other products may include thermal tapes, gaskets, and adhesives for heat management.</p></p>
