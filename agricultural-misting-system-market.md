<p><p>The agricultural misting system market is divided into two types: low pressure misting system and high pressure misting system. The low pressure misting system operates at a lower pressure level, typically below 250 psi, and produces larger water droplets. This system is often used for general cooling and humidification purposes. On the other hand, the high pressure misting system operates at a higher pressure level, usually ranging from 500 to 1000 psi, and produces finer water droplets. This system is typically employed for more specific applications such as greenhouse cooling or livestock cooling.</p></p>
