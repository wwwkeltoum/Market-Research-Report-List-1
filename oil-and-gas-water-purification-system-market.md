<p><p>The oil and gas water purification system market encompasses various types of technologies. Membrane processes involve passing the water through a semi-permeable membrane to remove impurities. Thermal treatment involves heating the water to evaporate and separate impurities. Ion exchange involves exchanging ions present in the water with desired ions to purify it. Capacitive deionization utilizes electrodes to remove ions from the water. These technologies play a crucial role in the oil and gas industry to ensure the quality and safety of water used in various operations, reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
