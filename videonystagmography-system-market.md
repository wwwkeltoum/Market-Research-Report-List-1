<p><p>The Videonystagmography (VNG) system market can be categorized into two types: wired and wireless. This configuration allows for accurate and real-time data transmission, ensuring reliability during testing. On the other hand, a wireless VNG system employs wireless communication technology to transmit data from the sensors and electrodes to the monitoring unit.
