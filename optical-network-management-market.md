<p><p>Optical Network Management Market Types refer to two specific technologies used in managing optical networks - Synchronous Optical Network (SONET) and Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM). SONET is a standardized protocol that allows multiple digital data streams to be transmitted simultaneously over optical fiber, optimizing network efficiency and reliability. On the other hand, WDM is a technique that enables multiple optical signals with different wavelengths to be combined and transmitted over a single fiber, increasing data capacity and enabling faster communication. These technologies play a vital role in effectively managing and enhancing optical network performance.</p></p>
