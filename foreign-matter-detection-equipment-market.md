<p><ul><li>Offline Detection Equipment</li><li>Online Detection Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foreign matter detection equipment market comprises two main types: offline detection equipment and online detection equipment. Offline detection equipment is used for inspecting products or materials in a separate and controlled environment, away from the production line. It allows for a more thorough examination and analysis of potential foreign matter. On the other hand, online detection equipment is integrated into the production line itself, providing real-time detection and removal of foreign matter during the manufacturing process, ensuring the quality and safety of the products.</p></p>
