<p><p>Medical isolation eye masks are protective devices worn over the eyes to prevent the spread of infectious diseases. There are two common types of these masks, namely tie-on and earloop masks. Tie-on masks have strings that can be tied behind the head, offering a secure fit and adjustable tension. On the other hand, earloop masks have elastic loops that go around the ears, providing a quick and convenient way to secure the mask in place. Both types effectively cover the eyes, minimizing the risk of transmission and protecting healthcare professionals and patients.</p></p>
