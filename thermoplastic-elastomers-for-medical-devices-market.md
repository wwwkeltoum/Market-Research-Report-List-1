<p><p>Thermoplastic elastomers (TPEs) are materials used in the manufacturing of medical devices. These TPEs can be divided into different types based on their composition. Styrene-based TPEs (SBCs) are commonly used and offer good flexibility and strength. Thermoplastic polyolefins are another type that provide good chemical resistance and durability. Thermoplastic polyurethanes are known for their excellent mechanical properties and high biocompatibility. Polyether Ester TPEs (TPEEs) are used in applications requiring superior chemical and heat resistance. Lastly, other types of TPEs exist, each with unique characteristics suitable for specific medical device requirements.</p></p>
