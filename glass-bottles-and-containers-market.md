<p><p>The glass bottles and containers market can be categorized into two types based on their appearance: transparent glass and opaque glass. Transparent glass bottles and containers are clear and allow the contents to be visible from the outside. They are often used for products where the aesthetics and visibility of the contents are important, such as liquor or cosmetic products. On the other hand, opaque glass bottles and containers are not transparent and prevent light from entering. They are commonly used for products that are light-sensitive and need protection from sunlight, such as medicines or certain food items.</p></p>
