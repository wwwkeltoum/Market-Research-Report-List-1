<p><p>In-Memory Database market types can be broadly categorized into Online Analytical Processing (OLAP) and Online Transaction Processing (OLTP). OLAP focuses on analyzing large sets of historical data to gain insights and make informed business decisions. OLTP, on the other hand, is designed for real-time transactional processing, handling high volumes of concurrent data modifications. Both types emphasize different aspects of database usage, with OLAP emphasizing reporting and analysis and OLTP focusing on real-time transaction processing.</p></p>
