<p><p>Analog gauges are devices used to measure and display various parameters in a visual format. The market for analog gauges can be categorized into three types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and others. Pneumatic analog gauges rely on air pressure to provide measurements, while hydraulic gauges use fluid pressure. Both types are commonly used in industrial applications. The "others" category includes gauges that utilize different mechanisms such as mechanical, electrical, or electronic methods to measure and display data. These gauges find applications in a wide range of industries, including automotive, aerospace, and healthcare.</p></p>
