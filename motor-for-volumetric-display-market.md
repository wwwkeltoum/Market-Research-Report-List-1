<p><strong>The Motor for Volumetric Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Light Processing (DLP) Technology</li><li>Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCOS) Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for volumetric display technology can be classified into two main types based on the motor used: Digital Light Processing (DLP) Technology and Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCOS) Technology. DLP technology utilizes an array of micro mirrors that tilt to reflect light, creating images with high resolution and color accuracy. LCOS technology, on the other hand, relies on liquid crystals to manipulate light and create images. Both technologies offer impressive visual displays for volumetric applications, but the underlying mechanisms differ.</p></p>
