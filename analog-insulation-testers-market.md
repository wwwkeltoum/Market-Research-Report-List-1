<p><p>Analog insulation testers are used to measure the insulation resistance in electrical systems. AC voltage testing refers to the measurement of the insulation resistance when an alternating current is applied to the system. This type of testing is particularly useful for determining the insulation condition in power distribution networks. On the other hand, DC voltage testing involves the measurement of insulation resistance when a direct current is applied. This type of testing is typically performed in industries such as telecommunications, where direct current systems are commonly used.</p></p>
