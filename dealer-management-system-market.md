<p><p>A Dealer Management System (DMS) is a software solution used by automotive dealerships to streamline their operations. There are two different market types for DMS: On-Premise and Cloud. On-Premise DMS refers to a system that is installed and operated on the dealership's own servers and infrastructure. It provides full control and customization but requires significant upfront investment and maintenance. On the other hand, Cloud DMS is a web-based solution hosted on remote servers. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as it eliminates the need for hardware purchases and provides regular updates and maintenance.</p></p>
