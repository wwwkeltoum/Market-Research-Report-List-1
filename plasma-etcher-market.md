They are equipped with advanced features such as self-cleaning mechanisms and automated loading and unloading of wafers. This ensures high efficiency and productivity. On the other hand, Semi-automatic Plasma Etchers require some manual intervention during the processing. Operators need to load and unload the wafers manually, but the processing itself is automated. These systems strike a balance between efficiency and flexibility, making them suitable for a variety of applications.</p></p>
