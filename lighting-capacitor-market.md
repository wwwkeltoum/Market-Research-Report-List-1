<p><p>The lighting capacitor market consists of two main types: dry type and oil-filled type capacitors. Dry type lighting capacitors do not contain any liquid and are designed for high voltage applications. They are reliable, compact, and require no maintenance. On the other hand, oil-filled type capacitors incorporate oil as an insulating medium. They are widely used in power systems due to their high energy storage capabilities. However, they require occasional maintenance to check for leaks and ensure proper functioning.</p></p>
