<p><p>Treatment chairs are widely used in healthcare settings and are available in two main types: electric and manual. Electric treatment chairs are equipped with a motorized mechanism that allows for effortless adjustment of the chair's position and height, providing convenience and ease of use for both patients and healthcare professionals.
