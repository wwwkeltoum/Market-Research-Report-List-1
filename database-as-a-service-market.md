<p><p>Database as a Service (DBaaS) is a cloud-based service that provides users with access to a managed database without the need for physical infrastructure or database management responsibilities. <p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premise</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Database as a Service (DBaaS) market includes two main types - the cloud-based market and the on-premise market. Cloud-based DBaaS involves hosting the database on a remote server, where the provider manages the infrastructure and maintenance. This offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, the on-premise market refers to databases managed locally by the business itself. Organizations maintain complete control over their data but must invest in infrastructure and maintenance. Both types serve the purpose of providing database management solutions, but differ in terms of location and management responsibilities.</p></p>
