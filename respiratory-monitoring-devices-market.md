<p><ul><li>Spirometry</li><li>Plethysmography</li><li>IOS/FOT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Respiratory monitoring devices are used to assess and monitor the functioning of the respiratory system. Spirometry is a common type of device that measures lung capacity and airflow, providing important insights into lung function. Plethysmography is another method that measures lung volumes and airway resistance, useful in diagnosing respiratory conditions. Impulse oscillometry (IOS) and forced oscillation technique (FOT) are non-invasive methods used to evaluate airway inflammation and resistance, offering a comprehensive assessment of respiratory health. These devices aid in diagnosing and managing respiratory conditions for improved patient care.</p></p>
