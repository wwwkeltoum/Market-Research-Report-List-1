<p><p>The market for Nano Screen Protectors can be divided into two types: Granular Film and Dense Film. On the other hand, Dense Film is a screen protector that is made from a denser material, providing enhanced durability and protection against scratches and impacts. Both types aim to safeguard electronic screens from damage while ensuring optimal visibility and touch sensitivity.</p></p>
