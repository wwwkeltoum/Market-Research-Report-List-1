<p><p>Laparoscopic ports refer to devices used in minimally invasive surgeries to create access points in the abdominal wall. There are two types of laparoscopic ports available in the market: multi-instrument and single-instrument. Multi-instrument ports are equipped with multiple channels, allowing the surgeon to insert several instruments simultaneously. On the other hand, single-instrument ports have a single channel, limiting the surgeon to use only one instrument at a time. These different types of ports provide flexibility and convenience during laparoscopic procedures, depending on the surgeon's requirements.</p></p>
