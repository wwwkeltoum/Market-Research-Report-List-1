<p><p>The marine beacons market applications can be classified into three main categories: offshore, coastal and harbor, and inland waters. Coastal and harbor beacons are deployed to assist ships in approaching ports and navigating narrow or congested waters. Inland water beacons are installed to aid vessels traveling through rivers, lakes, and smaller water bodies. Each type of marine beacon serves the purpose of enhancing navigational safety and reducing the risk of accidents in specific maritime environments.</p></p>
