<p><p>The Hollow Fiber Membrane Module Market is divided into two types: Ultrafiltration (UF) Membranes and Microfiltration (MF) Membranes. UF membranes are used for separating particles and molecules based on their size, allowing smaller molecules to pass through while retaining larger ones. This is utilized in various industries such as water treatment, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals. On the other hand, MF membranes have larger pore sizes and are mainly used for removing particles and bacteria from liquids in applications such as water purification and biotechnology.</p></p>
