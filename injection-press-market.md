<p><p>Injection press machines are used in a variety of industries for shaping materials such as plastics. There are three main types of injection presses: Electric, hydraulic, and hybrid. Electric presses use electric motors for all the machine's movements, resulting in high precision and energy efficiency. Hydraulic presses, on the other hand, rely on hydraulic fluid to generate power, offering high force and versatility. Hybrid presses combine the advantages of both electric and hydraulic machines, providing improved precision, energy savings, and increased productivity. These different types cater to the diverse needs of the injection press market.</p></p>
