<p><p>Semi-automatic beverage carton packaging machinery comes in various types to cater to different packaging needs. Horizontal end side-load machines are suitable for loading cartons sideways. Top-load machines are designed to load cartons from the top. Wraparound machines wrap cartons around products for secure packaging. Vertical leaflet machines insert leaflets into cartons vertically. Vertical sleeve machines add sleeves to cartons for extra protection. Each type offers unique features to meet specific packaging requirements in the beverage industry.</p></p>
