<p><p>The Photomask Repair Equipment Market encompasses three major types: Laser Technology, Focused Ion Beam (FIB) Technology, and Nanomachining Technology. Laser Technology employs lasers to remove the defects present on the photomasks, enhancing their quality and accuracy. On the other hand, Focused Ion Beam (FIB) Technology uses a focused beam of ions to perform precise repairs on the photomasks. Finally, Nanomachining Technology utilizes nanoscale machining techniques to eliminate defects, ensuring optimal functionality of the photomasks. These different technologies cater to the specific requirements of the photomask repair process.</p></p>
