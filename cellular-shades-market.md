<p><p>Cellular shades are a type of window treatment that consist of a honeycomb-shaped design for insulation benefits. Single cellular shades refer to shades with one layer of cells, offering basic insulation and light control. Double cellular shades have two layers of cells for enhanced insulation, preventing heat loss and improving energy efficiency. Other market types may include options like blackout cellular shades for total light blocking, sheer cellular shades for diffused light, and top-down/bottom-up cellular shades for flexible light control. These variations allow consumers to choose the cellular shade that best suits their preferences and needs.</p></p>
