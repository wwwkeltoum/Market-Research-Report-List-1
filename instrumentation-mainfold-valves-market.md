<p><p>The Instrumentation Mainfold Valves market includes two types: Direct Mounting and Remote Mounting. Direct Mounting refers to the installation of valves directly on the mainfold structure, eliminating the need for additional external components. This type offers simplicity and compactness, making it ideal for space-constrained environments. On the other hand, Remote Mounting involves positioning the valves away from the mainfold, connected by tubes or pipes. This type offers flexibility and ease of maintenance, allowing for remote operation and access to valves. Both types cater to different requirements and have their own advantages in the market.</p></p>
