<p><p>The adhesive papers market comprises two main types of printing techniques: flexographic printing and letterpress printing. Flexographic printing is a method that uses a flexible relief plate to transfer ink onto the paper. This technique is commonly used for high-volume printing due to its fast production rates. On the other hand, letterpress printing involves the direct impression of inked raised surfaces onto the adhesive paper. This technique is known for its distinctive high-quality, tactile feel, making it suitable for premium packaging and labeling applications.</p></p>
