<p><p>The Sulphur Analyzer market can be categorized into two types: Stationary Sulphur Analyzer and Portable Sulphur Analyzer. Stationary Sulphur Analyzers are fixed instruments used in laboratories or industrial settings for continuous monitoring of sulphur levels in various samples. On the other hand, Portable Sulphur Analyzers are handheld devices that allow for on-site analysis of sulphur content in different materials.
