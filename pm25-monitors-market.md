<p><ul><li>TEOM Monitor</li><li>Beta Attenuation Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PM2.5 monitors are essential tools for measuring fine particulate matter in the air. Two common types in the market are the TEOM Monitor and the Beta Attenuation Monitor. The TEOM Monitor uses a filter that collects particles and measures the change in their weight to determine air quality. On the other hand, the Beta Attenuation Monitor uses a beta radiation source to detect particles passing through a sensor. Both monitors provide valuable data for monitoring air pollution levels and ensuring public health and safety.</p></p>
