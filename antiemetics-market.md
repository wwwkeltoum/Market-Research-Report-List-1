<p><p>The antiemetics market encompasses various types of medications that help prevent or relieve nausea and vomiting. 5-HT3 receptor antagonists block the action of serotonin, reducing these symptoms. Dopamine antagonists inhibit dopamine receptors, effectively preventing nausea and vomiting. NK1 receptor antagonists work by blocking substance P, a neurotransmitter involved in these symptoms. Antihistamines act as H1 histamine receptor antagonists, reducing the response to histamine and relieving nausea. Cannabinoids, benzodiazepines, and anticholinergics also have antiemetic effects. Steroids help reduce inflammation and suppress the immune system, providing relief from nausea and vomiting.</p></p>
