<p><ul><li>Passive Filter</li><li>Active Filter</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High power filters are used to remove or reduce unwanted signals or noise from electrical circuits. Passive filters are simple and do not require an external power source. They use passive components like resistors, capacitors, and inductors to attenuate certain frequencies. Active filters, on the other hand, utilize active components like operational amplifiers to amplify or attenuate specific frequencies. They require an external power source for operation. The "Others" category may include specialized filters like digital filters or hybrid filters that combine passive and active components to achieve desired filtering characteristics.</p></p>
