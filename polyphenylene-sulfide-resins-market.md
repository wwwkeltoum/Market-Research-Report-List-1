<p><p>Polyphenylene Sulfide (PPS) resins are classified into three main types based on their structure and properties: Linear PPS, Cured PPS, and Branched PPS. Linear PPS resins have a simple linear structure and are commonly used in injection molding applications. Cured PPS resins are cross-linked to improve their thermal and chemical resistance, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Branched PPS resins have a more complex structure with branching points, offering a balance of properties between linear and cured PPS resins.</p></p>
