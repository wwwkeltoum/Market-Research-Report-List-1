<p><p>The Above-ground Outdoor Fire Hydrant System refers to a fire hydrant that is visibly installed on the ground, easily accessible for firefighters during emergencies. It is typically color-coded and comes with various fittings to connect hoses. On the other hand, the Underground Outdoor Fire Hydrant System is installed beneath the ground level, with only the visible outlet or access point above ground. This type of hydrant provides a discrete appearance while still allowing firefighters to quickly connect hoses during fire incidents. </p><p>Both systems are crucial components of fire safety infrastructure, ensuring efficient water supply for firefighting purposes.</p></p>
