<p><p>The Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Solenoid is a device that controls the flow of exhaust gas recirculation in the engine's intake manifold. The solenoid is responsible for opening and closing the EGR valve at the appropriate times, allowing the engine to meet emission standards while maintaining optimal performance.</p><p>The Exhaust Gas Recirculation Solenoid Market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
