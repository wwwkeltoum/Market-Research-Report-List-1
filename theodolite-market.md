<p><p>The theodolite market can be categorized into two main types: Optical Theodolite and Electronic Theodolite. Optical theodolites use lenses and a sighting telescope to measure angles and distances. They are often preferred for their accuracy and reliability in surveying and construction applications. On the other hand, electronic theodolites utilize electronic components to provide precise measurements quickly. They offer advanced features such as digital displays, data storage, and automatic angle calculations, making them more efficient and user-friendly. Both types of theodolites have their unique advantages and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>
