<p><p>Analytical Gel Permeation Chromatography (GPC) is a technique used for molecular weight determination of polymers. The GPC market can be categorized into two types: Ambient Temperature and High Temperature. Ambient Temperature GPC operates at normal room temperatures and is suitable for analyzing polymers with low thermal stability. On the other hand, High-Temperature GPC employs elevated temperatures for polymer analysis, enabling the study of polymers with high thermal stability. Both types of GPC offer researchers different options and flexibility to determine the molecular weight and related properties of various polymers.</p></p>
