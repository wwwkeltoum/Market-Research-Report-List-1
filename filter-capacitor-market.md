<p><p>Filter capacitors are electronic components used to remove unwanted noise or ripple from an electrical circuit. They can be classified into two types based on their application frequency: low frequency and high frequency market. Low frequency market filter capacitors are designed to filter out lower frequency signals, typically up to a few kilohertz. On the other hand, high frequency market filter capacitors are specifically engineered to remove high frequency noise, usually in the range of a few kilohertz to several gigahertz. These different types cater to specific frequency ranges and are utilized accordingly to ensure a smooth and efficient operation of electronic devices.</p></p>
