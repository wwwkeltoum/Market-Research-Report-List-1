<p><p>Skin closure surgery tissue adhesive is a type of adhesive used to close wounds or incisions during surgery. There are two main types of tissue adhesives in the market: polyurethane-based and cyanoacrylate-based. Polyurethane-based tissue adhesives are biocompatible and provide flexible and strong wound closure. Cyanoacrylate-based tissue adhesives have fast-acting bonding properties and create a secure seal. Both types offer different benefits depending on the surgical application, such as wound location and size. The choice of tissue adhesive depends on the surgeon's preference and the specific needs of the patient and the procedure.</p></p>
