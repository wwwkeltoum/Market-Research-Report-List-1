<p><p>Chloride ion selective electrodes are an essential tool for measuring chloride ion concentration in various samples. The market offers two types of calibration for these electrodes: high standard calibration and low standard calibration. High standard calibration involves calibrating the electrode using a solution with a known high chloride ion concentration. This calibration method ensures accuracy when measuring samples with high chloride ion levels. Conversely, low standard calibration involves using a solution with a known low chloride ion concentration. This calibration is suitable for accurately measuring samples with lower chloride ion levels.</p></p>
