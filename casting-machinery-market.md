<p><p>Casting machinery market consists of various types of machines used in different industrial processes. Metal casting machines are utilized to produce metal components by pouring molten metal into molds. Metal molding machines are used to shape metal materials into desired forms by applying pressure. Continuous coating machines are employed to coat metals with a protective layer for better durability. Die casting machines enable the production of complex metal parts by injecting molten metal into molds at high pressure. Other types of casting machinery include machines for centrifugal casting, vacuum casting, and investment casting, among others.</p></p>
