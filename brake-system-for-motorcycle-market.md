<p><p>The brake system for motorcycles consists of different market types including drum brake, disc brake, and linkage brake. </p><p>1. Drum brake: This type of brake uses brake shoes pressed against the inside of a rotating drum to slow down or stop the motorcycle.</p><p>2. Disc brake: This market type employs a caliper, which contains brake pads that squeeze against a disc (rotor) to generate friction and slow down the motorcycle.</p><p>3. Linkage brake: This system utilizes a combination of mechanical linkages to distribute the required force from the brake lever to the brakes on both the front and rear wheels of the motorcycle, providing balanced stopping power.</p></p>
