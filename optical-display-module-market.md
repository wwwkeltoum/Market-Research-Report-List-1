<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Optical Display Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prism Scheme</li><li>Curved Mirror (Worm Eye) Solution</li><li>Birdbath Scheme</li><li>Optical Waveguide Solution</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical display module market consists of different types such as the prism scheme, curved mirror (worm eye) solution, birdbath scheme, and optical waveguide solution. The prism scheme employs prisms to redirect light and create virtual images. The curved mirror (worm eye) solution uses a curved mirror to expand the field of view. The birdbath scheme utilizes two contoured mirrors to achieve a wide field of view. The optical waveguide solution projects images onto a waveguide, which then reflects them into the viewer's eyes.</p></p>
