<p><strong>The High Speed Inkjet Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Inkjet Printers</li><li>Drop-on-demand Inkjet Printers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High-speed inkjet printers are divided into two main types: continuous inkjet printers and drop-on-demand inkjet printers. Continuous inkjet printers continuously emit tiny droplets of ink onto the printing surface. These printers are ideal for high-speed and high-volume printing applications, offering consistent and efficient results. On the other hand, drop-on-demand inkjet printers release ink droplets only when necessary, ensuring precise and controlled printing. They are preferred for low to medium volume printing needs, allowing for higher quality and higher resolution prints.</p></p>
