<p><p>Gumboots are available in various market types, including PU, rubber, waterproof canvas, PVC, EVA, and others. PU gumboots are made with polyurethane material, while rubber gumboots are crafted using natural rubber, providing durability and flexibility. Waterproof canvas gumboots are lined with a waterproof layer to protect against water penetration. PVC gumboots are made from polyvinyl chloride, known for their waterproof and chemical-resistant properties. EVA gumboots utilize ethylene-vinyl acetate, offering lightweight and cushioning features. Lastly, "others" refers to different materials used in gumboot production, such as neoprene or leather.</p></p>
