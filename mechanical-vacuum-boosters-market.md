<p><strong>The Mechanical Vacuum Boosters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-sealed</li><li>Water-ring</li><li>DryVacuum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical vacuum boosters market comprises three main types: oil-sealed, water-ring, and dry vacuum. Oil-sealed boosters use oil as a sealing medium, which helps enhance performance and maintain a stable vacuum. Water-ring boosters use water as a sealing medium, providing efficient gas compression. Dry vacuum boosters, on the other hand, do not require any sealing fluid and operate by using rotating blades to compress gas. Each type offers distinct advantages and is used in various industrial applications depending on specific requirements.</p></p>
