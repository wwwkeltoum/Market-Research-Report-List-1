<p><p>Foundry equipment refers to various machines used in the metal casting process. Die casting machines are used to produce complex metal parts by injecting molten metal into a mold. Centrifugal casting machines use centrifugal force to create hollowed metal parts. Induction furnaces are used to melt metal using electromagnetic induction. Moulding machines are used to create molds for casting. Coremaking machines are used to produce cores for hollow parts. Shot blasting machines are used to remove impurities from castings using high-speed particles.</p></p>
