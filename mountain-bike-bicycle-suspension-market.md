<p><p>The mountain bike bicycle suspension market is divided into two main types: hardtail suspension and full suspension. Hardtail suspension refers to bikes with a front suspension fork, providing cushioning and control on rough terrains. These bikes do not have rear suspension, making them lighter and more efficient for pedaling. On the other hand, full suspension bikes have both front and rear suspensions, providing enhanced comfort and control on more challenging trails. These bikes are heavier but offer better traction, absorbing impacts to improve overall performance on uneven terrain.</p></p>
