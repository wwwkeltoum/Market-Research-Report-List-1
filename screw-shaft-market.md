<p><p>The Screw Shaft market consists of two main types: Internal Circulation Ballscrew and External Circulation Ballscrew. Internal Circulation Ballscrew is a type of screw shaft with recirculating balls inside the nut, which provides smooth motion and high precision. It is commonly used in applications where high speed and accuracy are required, such as CNC machines. On the other hand, External Circulation Ballscrew has recirculating balls within the screw shaft, offering enhanced load capacity and rigidity. It is typically employed in heavy-duty applications like industrial robots and aerospace equipment.</p></p>
