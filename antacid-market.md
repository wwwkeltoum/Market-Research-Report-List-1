<p><strong>The Antacid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Proton Pump Inhibitor</li><li>H2 Antagonist</li><li>Acid Neutralizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antacid market can be categorized into three types: proton pump inhibitors (PPIs), H2 antagonists, and acid neutralizers. PPIs work by reducing the production of stomach acid to provide relief from heartburn and other acid-related conditions. H2 antagonists block the histamine receptors, which decreases the production of stomach acid. Acid neutralizers act by directly neutralizing the excess stomach acid. These different types cater to varying levels of acidity and provide relief to individuals suffering from different forms of acid-related discomfort.</p></p>
