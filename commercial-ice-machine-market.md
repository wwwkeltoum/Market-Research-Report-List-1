<p><strong>The Commercial Ice Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-CooledIceMachine</li><li>Water-CooledIceMachine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial ice machines are commonly categorized into two types: air-cooled ice machines and water-cooled ice machines. Air-cooled ice machines use air to cool the refrigeration system, expelling heat to the surrounding environment. Conversely, water-cooled ice machines utilize water to cool the refrigeration system, dissipating heat through a water supply. Air-cooled ice machines tend to be more energy-efficient, cost-effective, and suitable for areas with good ventilation. On the other hand, water-cooled ice machines are often preferred in environments with limited airflow or higher ambient temperatures, ensuring efficient cooling performance.</p></p>
