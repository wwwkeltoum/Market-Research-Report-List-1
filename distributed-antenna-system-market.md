<p><p>A Distributed Antenna System (DAS) is a network of antennas that are strategically placed in an area to improve wireless network coverage and capacity. It consists of a central hub, fiber optic cables, and multiple antennas that are distributed throughout a building or an outdoor area. DAS is designed to enhance the performance of wireless communication by overcoming signal strength issues caused by obstacles like walls or other structures.</p><p>The Distributed Antenna System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for uninterrupted and high-quality wireless connectivity in various applications such as healthcare, transportation, industrial, and commercial sectors. These venues require robust wireless networks to cater to the increasing demand for data-intensive applications and services.</p><p>Furthermore, technological advancements such as the integration of small cells and remote radio heads with DAS solutions are expected to drive the market.
