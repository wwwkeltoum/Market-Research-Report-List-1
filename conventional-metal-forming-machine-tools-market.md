<p><strong>The Conventional Metal Forming Machine Tools Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bulk Deformation (BD)</li><li>Sheet Metal Working (SMW)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Conventional Metal Forming Machine Tools market is categorized into two types: Bulk Deformation (BD) and Sheet Metal Working (SMW). BD involves the manufacturing of metal components through processes like forging, extrusion, and rolling. It is typically used for producing large and sturdy metal parts. On the other hand, SMW focuses on the formation of metal sheets into desired shapes through processes like bending, cutting, and stamping. SMW is commonly used for producing smaller and more intricate metal components. Both types play a crucial role in the metal forming industry, catering to different manufacturing requirements.</p></p>
