<p><p>Oil particle counters are used to measure and monitor the presence of particles in oil samples. There are three main types of oil particle counters: Handheld particle counters, which provide portability and ease of use for on-site oil analysis; Desktop particle counters, which are larger and designed for laboratory analysis; and In-line particle counters, which are installed directly in oil systems to continuously monitor particle levels. Each type of particle counter has its own benefits and is suited for different applications depending on the specific requirements of the user.</p></p>
