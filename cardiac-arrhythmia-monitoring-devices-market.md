<p><p>Cardiac arrhythmia monitoring devices can be categorized into several types. Implantable cardiac monitors are small devices inserted under the skin to continuously monitor abnormal heart rhythms. Resting ECG devices and ECG stress test devices are used to record electrical activity of the heart at rest and during physical stress. Mobile cardiac telemetry monitors are portable devices that transmit real-time ECG data to healthcare providers remotely. Holter monitors are portable devices worn by patients for 24-48 hours to monitor heart rhythm continuously. These devices aid in monitoring and diagnosing cardiac arrhythmias.</p></p>
