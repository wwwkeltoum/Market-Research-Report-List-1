<p><p>The Commercial Storage Cabinet market consists of two main types: mobile and stable cabinets. Mobile cabinets are designed with wheels or casters, allowing for easy movement and repositioning. They are commonly used in office spaces, warehouses, and classrooms, where flexibility and portability are essential. On the other hand, stable cabinets are fixed and immovable once installed. These cabinets are typically used in manufacturing facilities, laboratories, and healthcare settings, where secure and stationary storage is required.
