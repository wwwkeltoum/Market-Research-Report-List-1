They are typically used for short-term or temporary monitoring requirements. On the other hand, stationary systems are installed at a fixed location and continuously monitor the environmental conditions in that particular area. They are suitable for long-term monitoring and provide real-time data for analysis and decision-making. Both types play a vital role in monitoring and assessing environmental factors.</p></p>
