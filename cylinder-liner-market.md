<p><p>The Cylinder Liner Market is categorized into two types: Dry Cylinder Liner and Wet Cylinder Liner. A Dry Cylinder Liner is a removable sleeve that directly contacts the piston and operates without any coolant. It is used in engines that generate less heat and do not require coolant circulation. On the other hand, a Wet Cylinder Liner is a sleeve that is surrounded by coolant and helps in cooling the engine by dissipating heat. It is commonly used in high-performance engines that generate a significant amount of heat.</p></p>
