<p><p>Metal Cleaning Equipment is available in two types: Solvent Metal Cleaning Equipment and Aqueous Metal Cleaning Equipment. The Solvent Metal Cleaning Equipment uses solvents to remove dirt, grease, and other contaminants from metal surfaces. It is suitable for heavy-duty cleaning applications, such as removing paint or rust. On the other hand, Aqueous Metal Cleaning Equipment uses water-based solutions, making it a safer and more environmentally friendly option. It is commonly used for general cleaning and less stubborn contaminants. Both types of equipment cater to different cleaning needs in the metal industry.</p></p>
