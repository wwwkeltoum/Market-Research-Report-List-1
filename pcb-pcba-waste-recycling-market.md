PCB recycling involves various stages like dismantling, sorting, crushing, and refining to recover these metals. On the other hand, PCBA (Printed Circuit Board Assembly) waste refers to the discarded electronic assemblies consisting of both PCBs and electronic components. PCBA recycling includes processes like dismantling, component removal, PCB recycling, and component recycling to recover valuable materials and minimize environmental impact. Both PCB and PCBA waste recycling aim to extract valuable resources, reduce pollution, and promote sustainable practices in the electronics industry.</p></p>
