<p><p>The drug screening market is divided into different types, including immunoassay analyzers, chromatography, breath analyzers, rapid testing devices, and assay kits and reagents. Immunoassay analyzers use antibodies to detect the presence of drugs in a person's urine, blood, or saliva. Chromatography separates drugs from a sample based on their properties. Breath analyzers measure alcohol or drug levels through breath samples. Rapid testing devices provide quick results for drug screening. Assay kits and reagents contain substances necessary for drug detection and analysis. These different types cater to various drug screening needs and preferences.</p></p>
