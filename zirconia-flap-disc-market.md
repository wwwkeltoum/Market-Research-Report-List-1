<p><p>Zirconia flap discs are abrasive tools used in grinding and polishing applications. They come in two main types: flat and conical. </p><p>Flat Zirconia flap discs have a flat surface and are suitable for smooth and consistent grinding operations on flat surfaces. They are commonly used for finishing and blending tasks.</p><p>Conical Zirconia flap discs have a cone-shaped design, allowing them to reach tight or curved areas that are difficult to access with flat discs. They are ideal for shaping, deburring, and smoothing irregular surfaces.</p><p>Both flat and conical Zirconia flap discs provide different advantages depending on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
