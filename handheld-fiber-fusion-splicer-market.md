<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Handheld Fiber Fusion Splicer market can be classified into two main types: Cladding Alignment and Core Alignment. Cladding Alignment splicers use a technique where both fiber claddings are aligned, ensuring efficient fusion of their cores. This method is suitable for single-mode fibers, but it may result in higher losses for multimode fibers. On the other hand, Core Alignment splicers ensure precise alignment of the fiber cores, minimizing losses and offering better performance for both single-mode and multimode fibers. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on specific requirements.</p></p>
