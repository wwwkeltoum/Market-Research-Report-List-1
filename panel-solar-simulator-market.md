<p><p>Panel solar simulators are devices used to replicate sunlight for testing solar panels. There are three types of panel solar simulators in the market. The continuous type provides a steady and constant output of light intensity, while the flashed type produces a short burst of light at high intensity. On the other hand, the pulsed type emits light pulses at regular intervals. Each type is designed to mimic different lighting conditions and is used for specific testing purposes in the solar panel industry.</p></p>
